The Blueprint — Core Philosophy

Toothbrush Therapy

Do the common uncommonly well.

Every successful trader has a set of habits they do without thinking. Not strategies. Not secrets. Just simple, repeatable actions done consistently — every session, every day. This is yours.

🎯
Know Your Bias
Before the open — is today a gap fill, a balance day, or a trend day? Know it before price moves.
📋
Have A Plan
"If price does X, I will do Y." Write it down. Say it out loud. Walk in with a roadmap, not a reaction.
♟️
Have A Second Plan
"If my first plan fails because X happens, I will do ABC." The market will surprise you. Be ready.
🎯
Set Your Process Goal
Pick one weakness. Set one goal. Say it out loud before you trade. "Today I will wait for confirmation before entering."
📖
Only Trade Your Setups
Not someone else's. Not a gut feeling. Yours. Refined. Trusted.
🛑
Honor Your Stop
Always. No exceptions. Ever.
📊
Review and Grade Every Trade
A+, B, or C. One line minimum. Every trade. Every day.
🤝
Find Your People
Find a trading partner or group who speaks the language and shares the passion. Talk about it regularly. The competition makes you better. The community keeps you honest.
🪥

When these eight habits become as automatic as brushing your teeth —
you will be well on your way to becoming
a disciplined trader.
